To Tatou Korero - Our Story

Haukai is a locally owned resturant which specializes in Māori kai and is based in the Bay of Islands It is owned by a couple who has a great focus on the community and those visting.

The owners would love for you to share and experience a meal, whether you are visiting or local. Our customers can experience Te Ao Māori with the use of karakia for blessing food and try our wonderfuk Kai and traditional Māori food
